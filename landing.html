What's in the box

TIERED PARRY

Three timing tiers — Perfect, Good, Late — with a chain meter that builds toward a free counter-special. Mash and you'll miss; time it and you'll heal on the chain.

10 STAGES, 4 BOSSES

From the neighborhood block through subway tunnels, the docks, an underground fight club, the freeway, and the penthouse rooftop where Kane has been waiting.

3 PROTAGONISTS

Each with a unique combo chain, signature special move, throw, launcher, and identity. Pick the fighter that fits the moment, not the one with the best stats.

DESKTOP / MOBILE / GAMEPAD

Plays in the browser, on the phone, with a Switch / Xbox / PS pad. One file, no installer. Touch controls and haptics on phones.

ACCESSIBILITY-FIRST

Reduced flash and shake toggles. Story / Normal / Brutal difficulty that scales damage taken so the genre stays approachable without gating bosses.

FAIR & HONEST

Small stakes. One neighborhood. We don't pretend you're saving the world — just the people on this corner. That's enough.
